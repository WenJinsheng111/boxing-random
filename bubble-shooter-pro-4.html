How to Play Bubble Shooter Pro 4

Game Rules

In Bubble Shooter Pro 4, your objective is to clear all bubbles from the board before they reach the bottom line. Aim your launcher and shoot colored bubbles to create groups of 3 or more matching bubbles, which will pop and disappear.

Each level has a specific layout of bubbles to clear. Some levels give you limited shots, so accuracy matters. Clearing bubbles that support other bubbles above them causes cascade drops, earning bonus points and clearing large sections at once.

Tips to Win at Bubble Shooter Pro 4

1

Aim for Clusters

Always target groups of bubbles rather than single ones. Popping a cluster of matching bubbles is far more efficient and clears the board faster than picking off individual bubbles one at a time.

2

Use Wall Bounces

Bounce your bubbles off the side walls to reach tricky angles and hard-to-access areas. Mastering bank shots is essential for clearing bubbles hidden behind other colors in later levels.

3

Clear from the Top

Focus on clearing bubbles near the top of the board whenever possible. Removing support bubbles causes entire sections below them to drop, creating massive chain reactions and clearing large portions at once.

4

Save Power-Ups

Don't waste special power-up bubbles on easy shots. Save bomb, rainbow, and lightning bubbles for tough situations where they can make the biggest impact and turn a losing board into a win.
