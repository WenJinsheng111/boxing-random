How to Play Cool Supercars Stunts PVP

Tips to Win at Cool Supercars Stunts PVP

1

Build Speed Before Ramps

Use straight sections of the track to build up maximum speed before hitting ramps. The faster you are when you launch, the higher and farther you'll fly, giving you more airtime for complex stunt combinations.

2

Use Handbrake for Sharp Turns

The handbrake is essential for navigating tight corners without losing too much speed. Tap it briefly while turning to initiate a controlled drift that keeps your momentum going through the curve.

3

Balance Your Car Mid-Air

When airborne, use the steering controls to balance your car for a clean landing. Landing on all four wheels preserves your speed and keeps your combo alive. A bad landing can cost you the race.

4

Learn the Track Layout

Spend time learning each track's layout to find optimal racing lines. Knowing where the ramps, turns, and shortcuts are located gives you a huge advantage over opponents who are navigating blind.
