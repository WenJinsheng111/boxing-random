What is M5 City Driver?

The game delivers an authentic driving experience with carefully tuned car physics that capture the power and handling of one of the world's most celebrated performance sedans.

The city itself is a character in the game, featuring busy intersections with functioning traffic lights, highways with flowing traffic, residential neighborhoods, industrial districts, and downtown areas packed with skyscrapers. Sandbox mode lets you cruise wherever you want with no restrictions, while mission mode challenges you with time-limited deliveries, checkpoint races through city streets, and precision parking challenges that test your skills to the limit.
