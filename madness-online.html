Tips to Survive in Madness Online

1

Gather Resources Early

Don't rush into combat right away. Spend the first few minutes scavenging for resources and materials. A well-stocked inventory gives you a huge advantage when enemies start swarming.

2

Build Your Base Strategically

Place your base in a defensible position with limited entry points. Use walls and barriers to funnel enemies into kill zones where you can take them out efficiently.

3

Master the Dodge

The Spacebar dodge is your best survival tool. Time your dodges to avoid enemy attacks, especially during intense firefights. A well-timed dodge can save your life.

4

Team Up in Co-op

Playing with teammates makes survival much easier. Assign roles — one player gathers resources while another defends the base. Communication and coordination are key to lasting longer.
