How to Play Boxing Stars

Game Rules

Boxing Stars is a head-to-head boxing match where two players fight in the ring. Select your fighter and use a combination of punches and defensive moves to deplete your opponent's health bar. Land clean hits to deal damage, and string together combos for maximum impact.

The match continues until one player's health is fully depleted, resulting in a knockout. Manage your stamina carefully — throwing wild punches leaves you open to devastating counter-attacks.

Tips to Win at Boxing Stars

1

Master the Jab

The jab is your most reliable tool. It is fast, keeps your opponent at range, and sets up bigger punches. Use it to control the pace of the fight and interrupt your opponent's attacks before they land.

2

Block Then Counter

Patience wins fights. Block your opponent's attack and immediately counter with a hook or uppercut while they are recovering. Counter-punches deal extra damage and catch opponents off guard.

3

Know Your Fighter

Each fighter has unique strengths. Speed fighters should stay mobile and land quick combos, while power fighters should look for one big opening. Play to your character's advantages.

4

Manage Your Distance

Footwork is everything in boxing. Stay at a range where your punches connect but your opponent's miss. Step in for combos and step out before they can retaliate.
