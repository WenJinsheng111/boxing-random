How to Play Basket Random

Game Rules

Basket Random is a best-of-9 basketball duel. Two players face off on a basketball court, and the goal is simple: score a basket before your opponent does. Each successful basket scores one point, and the first player to reach 5 points wins the match.

After every round, the game randomizes everything — player heights, jump power, ball size, court surface, and even gravity. You need to quickly assess the new conditions and adapt your strategy on the fly.

Controls

Player Control Action
Player 1 W Jump / Shoot
Player 2 ↑ (Up Arrow) Jump / Shoot

One button per player — that's it! Jump to grab the ball and shoot for the basket. The physics engine handles your player's movement, making every attempt uniquely chaotic.

Tips to Win at Basket Random

1

Assess the Round First

Don't jump immediately. Take a second to see your player's size, jump height, and court conditions. Understanding the round's physics gives you a major advantage over opponents who button-mash.

2

Time Your Jumps

Jumping at the right moment is crucial. Try to jump when the ball is near the basket or when you can intercept your opponent's shot. Well-timed jumps win more rounds than frantic pressing.

3

Use Your Body

Your ragdoll player's body can block shots and redirect the ball. Sometimes the best defense is positioning your player between your opponent and the basket.

4

Adapt Your Strategy

When you're a tall player, play aggressively near the basket. When you're short, focus on timing and interceptions. The randomness demands flexibility — rigid strategies lose.
