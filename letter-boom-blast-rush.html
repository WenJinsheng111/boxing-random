How to Play Letter Boom Blast Rush

Game Rules

Letter Boom Blast Rush presents you with a grid of letter blocks. Your goal is to find and form valid words by connecting adjacent letters. Longer words score more points and create bigger explosions on the board.

In timed mode, you must reach the target score before time runs out. In endless mode, keep forming words to prevent the board from filling up. Chain reactions occur when explosions from one word reveal new matching opportunities, leading to massive bonus scores.

Controls

Input Control Action
Select Mouse Select and connect letters
Submit Click Submit formed words

Click and drag your mouse across adjacent letter tiles to form words. Release to submit the word. Valid words will explode and clear from the board, while invalid words will simply deselect.

Tips to Win at Letter Boom Blast Rush

1

Go for Long Words

Longer words create much bigger explosions and higher scores. Instead of quickly submitting 3-letter words, take a moment to trace longer paths through the letter grid for maximum impact and chain potential.

2

Plan Chain Reactions

Look at what letters sit above or around your target word. Clearing one word may cause other letters to fall into new positions, creating opportunities for follow-up combos that multiply your score.

3

Focus on Vowel Clusters

Areas of the board with multiple vowels nearby are goldmines for word formation. Scan for vowel-rich regions first, then look for consonants around them to build high-scoring words quickly.

4

Use Special Tiles Wisely

Multiplier and bonus tiles can dramatically boost your score. Try to include them in your longer words rather than wasting them on short, low-scoring words. Time their use for maximum effect.
