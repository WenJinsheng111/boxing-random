Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best casual party games for groups?

MiniBattles and Mini Duels Battle are excellent for two players with their rapid-fire mini-game format. For larger groups of up to 6 players, Mini Heads Party is the top choice. All games feature short rounds and simple controls that anyone can learn in seconds.

How many players can play these party games?

Most of our casual games support 2 players on one keyboard. Mini Heads Party supports up to 6 players sharing one device, making it perfect for larger groups. Solo skill games like Ragdoll Flip and Knife Master are great for single-player fun too.

Do I need to download anything to play these games?

No downloads are needed. All casual and party games run directly in your web browser. Just click on any game and start playing instantly. They work on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and other modern browsers on both desktop and mobile devices.
