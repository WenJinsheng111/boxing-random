How to Play Ragdoll Flip

Game Rules

Ragdoll Flip challenges you to perform acrobatic flips with a ragdoll character and land safely. Click or tap to launch your character into the air and control the rotation. The more flips you complete before landing, the higher your score.

The key to success is timing your jumps and rotations perfectly. Land on your feet for a clean landing and bonus points. Land on your head or back for a hilarious wipeout and zero points for that attempt.

Controls

Click or tap to launch your ragdoll into the air. The timing and duration of your input determines the height and rotation of your flip. Release at the right moment to stick the landing.

Tips to Master Ragdoll Flip

1

Start with Single Flips

Master the single flip before attempting doubles or triples. Learn the exact timing needed to complete one full rotation and land cleanly. Once single flips feel natural, gradually add more rotations.

2

Watch Your Rotation Speed

Pay attention to how fast your ragdoll is spinning. If the rotation is too fast, you might over-rotate and crash. If it is too slow, you will not complete the flip. Adjust your timing to control rotation speed.

3

Focus on the Landing

A spectacular flip means nothing if you face-plant the landing. Always prioritize completing flips that you can land cleanly. A safe single flip scores more than a failed triple flip attempt.

4

Use Height to Your Advantage

Higher jumps give you more airtime and room for extra rotations. Learn to maximize your launch height with well-timed inputs, then use that extra time to squeeze in additional flips.
