Tips to Win at Mixed Macho Arts

1

Learn Basic Combos

Start by mastering simple two-hit combos before attempting longer chains. A punch followed by a kick is a reliable combination that deals solid damage and is easy to execute under pressure.

2

Mix Up Your Attacks

Do not rely on a single type of strike. Alternate between punches, kicks, and special moves to keep your opponent guessing. Predictable fighters are easy to counter and block.

3

Use Movement Wisely

Positioning is crucial in the octagon. Circle around your opponent to find angles for attacks and avoid being cornered against the cage. Good footwork creates opportunities that raw power cannot.

4

Save Specials for Openings

Special moves are powerful but can leave you vulnerable if they miss. Wait for your opponent to whiff an attack or drop their guard before unleashing your special for maximum impact.
